# What is Job Mastery?

## Rotation
- The optimal sequence of abilities to maximize damage or healing.

## Openers
- Burst sequences performed at the start of fights to align with party buffs.

## Resources
- Guides like **The Balance Discord** provide detailed breakdowns.
